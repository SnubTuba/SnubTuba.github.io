Leap Year Ball

Their Majesties invite you to their castle on the Second of March 2024.


Guests are encouraged to wear clothing styles pre-dating the 1600's.
Think outfits typically found at Ren Fests. Clothing can be from any regions in the world, not just European. Keep in mind cooler weather when preparing.

A Device is the symbol you or your household is known for. You'll be creating a shield by selecting the colors, patterns, and charges you'd like to display on your device.
For printing purposes, please only select the colors Argent, Or, Gules, Azure, Vert, Sable, and Purpure. Please DO NOT add on extra decorations outside of the shield.
You are more than welcome to adjust your device how you see fit in another program. Please keep all colors flat.
